### 教育理念

```dataview
TABLE rows.file.link AS 考題
FROM #題目
WHERE contains(file.name, "教育理念")
FLATTEN file.outlinks AS Links
GROUP BY link(meta(Links).display) AS 主題
SORT length(rows.考題) DESC
```
